FAQ

Questions,
answered.

When will it launch?
We're in the final stretch of development. Launch details will be announced on this page as soon as they're set.
What kinds of bookings can it import?
Flights, hotels, restaurants, trains, activities — any confirmation email or PDF. Both Japanese and English confirmations are supported.
Does it work offline?
Yes. Your itinerary and the original documents (emails, PDFs, images) are stored on your device, so everything stays readable in airplane mode or abroad without data. An internet connection is needed for AI import and day-plan generation.
Where is my data stored?
Your itinerary lives on your device and in your own iCloud only. The AI extraction service processes the text you send and does not store your itinerary on any server.
Can I share trips with family?
Yes. iCloud sharing lets your family or partner see the same itinerary. When someone adds a booking, it appears on everyone's timeline.
How is Tripfy related to Menufy?
Tripfy is a sibling app of Menufy, the menu-translation app by the same developer. Menufy handles what you eat on the road; Tripfy handles where you're going.
